As a Customer
I can know how long should I wait for ice cream so that I didn't stand in the queue.
    
    Requirements
        1) The product shall show that time of ice cream is finished.
        2) The product shall confirm customer to take ice cream in time.
        3) The product shall notify the customer in their smartphone.

As a Customer
I can order ice cream from anywhere I want so that I can take ice scream right away when it's time.

    Requirements
        1) The product shall find ice cream that has in the shop.
        2) The product shall take an order of ice cream from the application.
        3) The product shall update the queue real-time as soon as order finish.
